## Idea behind this

[Grocerly](https://github.com/SuhasDissa/Grocerly) is an AI powerd grocery shopping app, that we built for AgenTrix 2026. Personally I'm a very lazy person, and I hate going out of the house specially when it feels like a waste of time. What I mostly do is use Uber Eats. In uber eats I can pick a store I like, and add whatever I need to my basket and order that. I don't even have to pay any delivery fee either, because of my Uber One subscription. (Seriously, I can literally save money by using uber eats compared to going shopping myself).

Anyway, that wasn't good enough for us. That's why we built Grocerly. It behaves exactly like uber eats, but the way of browsing the store and picking the product is better. Instead of scrolling through product listings for 20minutes searching for what you need, you just ask the agent, "Hey, find me some bananas" and the agent will do the hard work for you, and show you some bananas. You can pick them from your favourite shop, or you can pick them for cheap from another shop.

![Grocerly Platform](/images/projects/grocerly/image-2.png)

## Overview

The interface Is quite simple. When you first go-to our website, you will see a giant text box. Remember the time when Cursor was introducecd, and all you see was a giant textbox to enter a prompt, and the file editor was nowhere to be found?, and Grocerly gives you the same feeling, but for Grocery shopping

There's a chatbot like interface, where you can ask for stuff, and agent will simply pull out the stuff you need

![Grocerly Platform](/images/projects/grocerly/image-3.png)

## Features

- **AI Meal Planning**: You can use the chat intereface to also ask casual questions and do meal planning and stuff
- **Basket Optimization**: Agent will check for product pricings and optimize your bucket so that you can get the cheapest deals, while going to least amount of shops
- **Pantry feature**: You can also have a pantry like thing on this app, basically, this acts like a wishlist, but for grocery items. You can ask the agent to add products to pantry, or even upload a reciept to put those items to pantry

## Tech Stack

TypeScript, Bun, Elysia.js, React 19, Gemini 2.5 Flash, Vertex AI, SQLite, FTS5, Drizzle ORM, WebSockets, Docker
